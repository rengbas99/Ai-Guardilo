🛡️ AI Guardrail: The Ultimate UK PII Shield for AI

AI Guardrail automatically detects sensitive UK personal data as you type or paste into AI assistants, enterprise tools, and web forms—empowering you to redact it with a single click before it ever leaves your browser.

🏆 MILESTONE REACHED: Proudly protecting users across the UK healthcare, legal, and HR sectors!

🔍 WHAT IT DETECTS

- Physical Addresses: UK street names, house numbers, and apartment details.
- Full Names: Intelligent detection with context awareness.
- National Identity: Full support for National Insurance (NI) and NHS Numbers (with Mod-11 validation).
- Financial Data: Combined Sort Code + Account Number detection and Payment Card validation.
- Contact Info: Intelligent email and UK phone number detection (+44 and 07xxx).
- Official IDs: UK Passport, Driving Licence, and VAT registration numbers.
- Location: Precise UK Postcode matching.
